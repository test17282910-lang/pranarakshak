# 🌟 Pranarakshak Unique Features & Roadmap

## ✅ Current Unique Features:
1. **AI-Powered 24h AQI Forecasting** - LSTM model with Monte Carlo dropout
2. **Personalized Health Risk Assessment** - Considers COPD, Asthma severity
3. **Condition-Specific Precautions** - Custom advice based on symptoms
4. **Beautiful Earth Visualization** - Animated globe with atmospheric effects
5. **WhatsApp Sandbox Alerts** - Free international messaging
6. **Smart Rate Limiting** - Prevents alert spam (12h cooldown)

## 🚀 Suggested Unique Additions:

### 1. **Smart Indoor Air Quality Recommendations**
- **Feature**: Suggest best times to open windows, run air purifiers
- **Value**: Optimize indoor air based on outdoor predictions
- **Implementation**: Add `/indoor-recommendations` endpoint

### 2. **AQI Photo Evidence**
- **Feature**: Let users upload photos of hazy/clear skies to validate predictions
- **Value**: Crowd-sourced validation of AI predictions
- **Implementation**: Add photo upload to dashboard

### 3. **Medication Reminder Integration** 
- **Feature**: Remind users to take preventive inhalers on high AQI days
- **Value**: Proactive health management
- **Implementation**: Add medication schedule to user profile

### 4. **Family Group Alerts**
- **Feature**: Alert family members when elderly/vulnerable relatives are at risk
- **Value**: Community health protection
- **Implementation**: Add family group feature with shared alerts

### 5. **AQI Gamification**
- **Feature**: "Clean Air Streak" - reward users for good air quality days
- **Value**: Engagement and positive reinforcement
- **Implementation**: Add points/badges system

### 6. **Voice Alerts (Text-to-Speech)**
- **Feature**: WhatsApp voice messages for critical alerts
- **Value**: Accessibility for visually impaired users
- **Implementation**: Integrate with Twilio voice API

### 7. **Smart Route Planning**
- **Feature**: Suggest cleanest routes between locations
- **Value**: Minimize exposure during travel
- **Implementation**: Integrate with Google Maps API + AQI data

### 8. **Emergency Contact Auto-Alert**
- **Feature**: Automatically alert emergency contacts during "Critical" AQI events
- **Value**: Safety net for vulnerable users
- **Implementation**: Add emergency contacts to user profile

### 9. **Wearable Device Integration**
- **Feature**: Send alerts to smartwatches (Apple Watch, Fitbit)
- **Value**: Instant notifications during outdoor activities
- **Implementation**: Use device notification APIs

### 10. **AI Health Coach Chatbot**
- **Feature**: WhatsApp chatbot that answers air quality questions
- **Value**: 24/7 health guidance
- **Implementation**: Integrate with OpenAI API + medical knowledge base
